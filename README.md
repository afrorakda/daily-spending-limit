# Daily Spending Limit
A hyper-minimalist tool to calculate your "survival budget" instantly. 

## 🚀 Concept: 1 Tool / 1 Action
How much can I spend today? This tool gives you the answer in 3 seconds. No complex charts, no categorized tracking—just the one number you need to survive until your next payday.

## ✨ Features
- **Zero Friction:** No login. No tracking. No ads. Just a tool.
- **Instant Result:** Large, bold display of your daily limit.
- **Currency Agnostic:** Works with any unit ($, €, ¥, RM, etc.) and remembers it for your next visit.
- **Privacy First:** All data stays in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded.
- **PWA Ready:** Optimized for "Add to Home Screen" on iOS and Android.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Unit:** Enter your currency symbol (optional).
2. **Remaining Budget:** Enter the total money you have left.
3. **Days Left:** Enter the number of days until your next income.
4. **Action:** Your daily spending limit is instantly calculated and displayed.

## 📐 The Philosophy
Financial freedom starts with knowing your limits. By visualizing exactly what you can afford "per day," you gain the mental clarity to avoid overspending and make it to the end of the month with confidence.
